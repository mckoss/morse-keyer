Typical QSO

You: CQ CQ DE your callsign your callsign K

Them: your callsign DE their callsign their callsign K

You: GM their callsign TNX FER CALL

You: UR RST 599

You: QTH your city

You: NAME your name

You: HW?

You: AR their callsign DE your callsign K (or simply BK)
